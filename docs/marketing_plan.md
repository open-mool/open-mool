# Open Mool Launch Checklist

## Phase 1: Repository Readiness
- [x] **Documentation**: Finalize README, LICENSE (MIT), and Whitepaper. (Completed)
- [ ] **Contribution Guidelines**: Ensure `CONTRIBUTING.md` creates a clear path for new developers.
- [ ] **Issue Triage**: Populate 5-10 tickets with "good-first-issue" labels for new contributors.
- [ ] **Code of Conduct**: Add standard `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` to ensure a safe community space.

## Phase 2: Community Outreach
- [ ] **Technical Announcement**: Share the repository on relevant open-source forums (e.g., r/opensource, Hacker News) focusing on the "Edge + AI" stack.
- [ ] **Public Building**: Share development updates on Twitter/X using #BuildInPublic.
- [ ] **Technical Blog**: Publish an article on "Building a low-carbon archival architecture" to attract engineering talent.

## Phase 3: Contributor Onboarding
- [ ] **First Guardians**: Manually invite 10 individual contributors (travelers/photographers) to test the upload flow.
- [ ] **Feedback Channel**: Establish a place for user feedback (GitHub Discussions or Discord).
- [ ] **Recognition**: Implement the automated "Scout" badge for first-time uploaders.

## Phase 4: Institutional Partnerships
- [ ] **Identify Partners**: List 5 cultural organizations or NGOs in the Himalayan region.
- [ ] **Initial Contact**: Reach out to offer free digitization and archival tools.
- [ ] **Pilot Program**: Onboard one partner organization to upload a seed collection (100GB+).
